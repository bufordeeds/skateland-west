## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need to make a reservation for regular skating sessions?**
No reservations needed for public sessions - just show up! However, we recommend calling ahead on busy nights and holidays.

**Can I bring my own skates?**
Yes! We just need to inspect them for safety and floor compatibility at the front desk.

**Do you offer group discounts?**
Yes! Groups of 15 or more receive discounts. Call us for pricing.

**What's your cancellation policy for parties?**
Full refund with 14+ days notice, 50% refund with 7-13 days notice. See our private events page for full details.

**Do you have parking?**
Yes! Free parking lot with plenty of spaces right in front of our building.

**Are outside decorations allowed?**
Yes for private events and party rooms. Please check with staff for specific guidelines.
