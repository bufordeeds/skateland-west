### 13. FAQ

**Content type:** Text (only fully text-based page on the site)

**Q&A pairs:**

1. **Do you charge for Parents?** — Yes, everyone pays to come into the building. Non-skating parents (coming in at the same time as their children) — no charge at that time. Coming in later without child — everyone pays.

2. **If my friends are coming to skate, do I need to pay if I'm not skating?** — Everyone entering the building pays.

3. **Do you allow in and out privileges?** — No. When you leave, you leave. If you want to come back in, pay admission again.

4. **Do you sell food?** — Yes! Slushies, Personal Sized Pizza, ALL BEEF Hot Dogs, Pretzels & Ricos Nachos. Pepsi products.

5. **Can I bring in food from the outside?** — No outside food or drink. Only exception: birthday cake during open skating session with a Public Birthday Party reservation.

6. **Do you have a dress code?** — Yes. Neat and clean. No suggestive/vulgar language on clothes. No bare clothing, tank tops, under shirts, cut off shirts. Shirts fully closed, no open backs or bare stomachs. No hats in the building. Strictly enforced.

7. **Can we chew gum, or smoke?** — NO. No gum chewing or smoking of any kind.

8. **Do you sell gift certificates?** — Yes, at the front office.

9. **Do you take requests for songs?** — Yes, music library with over 7,000 songs. Floor Host will make every effort to play your favorite song.

10. **Can I rent Skateland West privately?** — Yes! Exclusive Events on select days. Check Parties page for more info.

11. **How far in advance do I need to make party reservations?** — Open Events: at least 2 weeks. Exclusive Events: 30 days to 6 months, depending on availability. No last minute parties.

12. **What forms of payments do you allow?** — Cash Only!

13. **Can I pay with a check?** — No personal checks.

14. **What size skates do you rent?** — Juvenile size 7 to adult size 15.

15. **Can I bring my own skates?** — Yes. All kinds of regular roller skates and inline skates. Must be safe and clean.

16. **Can I wear my Heelys?** — No heely type skates, hover boards, skateboards, or other non-approved equipment.

17. **Can my child wear FP Grow with me skates?** — No. No skates where you put the child's shoe into the skate.

18. **Do you sell skates?** — Yes!

19. **If my child doesn't skate well, can I walk on the floor?** — During open skate, parents cannot enter the skating floor with shoes on to help children.

20. **Where can I get skating lessons?** — Right here at Skateland West. Every Saturday from 1:30-2:00pm!

## Key Observations

### Content that differs from new site
- **Pricing is detailed** — Old site has specific dollar amounts for all packages and sessions. New site has placeholder/empty package sections.
- **Schedule is current** — Old site schedule was updated Feb 2026. New site schedule data is incomplete.
- **Party packages are comprehensive** — 4 distinct packages with clear pricing, inclusions, add-ons, and rules. New site has empty PartyPackages blocks.
- **FAQ is substantial** — 20 Q&A pairs covering policies, dress code, food, payments. New site has no FAQ page.
- **Cash only policy** — Important detail not mentioned on new site.

### Content to migrate to new CMS
1. All 4 party package details (pricing, inclusions, add-ons, rules)
2. Skating schedule with prices
3. Skate lessons info + the free skating perk
4. Skate sales info (Sure-Grip, Riedell brands)
5. FAQ content (20 Q&A pairs)
6. Fitness facts infographic content
7. Rink photos (download originals from WordPress media library)
8. Welcome/about text
9. Dress code policy
10. Food menu items and pricing

### Content format issues on old site
- Almost all content is in **image flyers** rather than text — bad for SEO, accessibility, and mobile
- The new Payload CMS site should convert all flyer content to structured text fields
- Only the FAQ and homepage welcome text are actual HTML text
