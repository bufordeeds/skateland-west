# Skateland West Website Redesign Roadmap

## Project Overview
Transform Skateland West's outdated WordPress site into a modern, mobile-first platform that drives party bookings and increases revenue through improved user experience and conversion optimization.

### Current State Assessment
Based on analysis of the legacy website:
- **Design**: Early 2000s aesthetic with clip art and amateur graphics
- **Mobile**: Non-responsive, desktop-only design
- **UX**: Confusing navigation, information overload, poor content hierarchy
- **Conversion**: Weak CTAs, complex booking process, unclear pricing
- **Brand**: Inconsistent visual identity across pages

### Success Metrics
- Increase online party bookings by 300%
- Mobile conversion rate >5%
- Page load speed <2 seconds
- Bounce rate <40%
- Average session duration >3 minutes

## Resource Requirements

### Team Composition
- **Project Manager**: Overall coordination and stakeholder communication
- **UX/UI Designer**: Design system, wireframes, and visual design
- **Frontend Developer**: React/Next.js implementation
- **Backend Developer**: API and database development
- **Content Strategist**: Messaging and content creation
- **SEO Specialist**: Search optimization
- **Photographer**: Professional facility and event photos
- **QA Tester**: Quality assurance and testing

### Technology Stack
- **Frontend**: Next.js, React, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS
- **Backend**: Node.js, PostgreSQL/MySQL
- **CMS**: Sanity or Contentful
- **Hosting**: Vercel or AWS
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics 4, Hotjar
- **Email**: SendGrid or Postmark
- **Payments**: Stripe or Square

### Budget Considerations
- Professional photography: $2,000-3,000
- Design and development: $40,000-60,000
- Content creation: $3,000-5,000
- Third-party services: $200-500/month
- Marketing launch campaign: $5,000-10,000

## Risk Mitigation

### Potential Risks
1. **Scope creep**: Clearly defined requirements and change management process
2. **Technical challenges**: Experienced development team and thorough planning
3. **Content delays**: Early content planning and multiple review cycles
4. **Integration issues**: Early testing of third-party services
5. **Launch issues**: Comprehensive testing and staged rollout

### Contingency Planning
- 10% buffer time built into each phase
- Weekly progress reviews
- Clear escalation procedures
- Rollback plan for launch
- Post-launch support team ready
