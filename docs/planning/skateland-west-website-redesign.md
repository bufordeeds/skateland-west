# Skateland West Website Redesign - Complete Strategy

## Executive Summary

Transform Skateland West from an outdated WordPress site into a modern, mobile-first platform that drives party bookings and increases revenue through improved user experience and conversion optimization.

#### **Current Issues:**

-   Dated 3D graphics and potentially divisive slogan
-   Text-heavy content that's hard to scan
-   No clear visual hierarchy or calls-to-action
-   Poor mobile experience
-   Buried booking functionality

**Hero Section:**
